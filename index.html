Research Projects

My research interests are in the domain of multitask learning, dimension reduction techniques for scaling up machine learning, application of machine learning in natural language processing. Some of my ongoing projects are

Multitask Learning using Compact RAM model

This project tries to solve a high dimensional multitask learning with a limited RAM.

Dimension Reduction using Cuckoo hashing

In this project we are trying to propose a variant of Cuckoo Hashing which can be directly applied to reduce high dimensional machine learning tasks.

During my internship in Indian Statiscal Institute ,Kolkata in the year of 2014, I have done some work of computer vision also.
